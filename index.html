About the Game

Crownless is a hand‑crafted, 2D Metroidvania platformer set deep beneath the waves. Explore labyrinthine ruins and forgotten caverns, harness powerful abilities and overcome relentless enemies as you journey to reclaim a kingdom lost beneath the sea. Developed by Studio Wallnut, Crownless combines tight platforming, atmospheric storytelling and a gorgeous visual style to deliver a new adventure for fans of the genre.

Features

Explore a Sunken World

Discover winding tunnels, ancient temples and hidden secrets in a richly interconnected world.

Epic Boss Battles

Face fearsome guardians and monstrous creatures, each with unique attack patterns and strategies.

Unlock New Paths

Earn powerful upgrades to traverse previously unreachable areas and unravel the mysteries of the abyss.
